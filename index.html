All new Audi Q3

Experience the Augmented Reality

Exterior

With its dynamic overall appearance, the Audi Q3 exudes urban lifestyle.

Quintessential Car

The front end is dominated by the large Singleframe grille with vertical   struts. Connected by the shoulder line, the headlights and rear lights form a single design element. A palette of 5 paint ﬁnishes are available, including pulse orange.

Audi Q3 Experience 360°

Spacious and variable

You can electrically adjust the seats to your liking. Thanks to the long wheelbase, you and your passengers can enjoy a wealth of head and knee room.

Reorganisation-made simple:You can adjust the fore/aft position of the rear seat bench by 15 centimetres. Depending on the position of the rear seat bench and backrests, you have up to 530 liters of luggage capacity at your disposal.When the backrests are folded down,that goes up to 1,525 litres

Master of all tasks

You can opt to inﬂuence the characteristics of your vehicle depending on the driving situation, road condition or your own needs with the Audi drive select.
